About Olanrewaju

Olanrewaju Idris is a Senior Finance Analyst with Neukleos Marketing Services.
Olanrewaju is a self-driven and motivated individual who has committed himself to constant self-development. Consequently, he has acquired knowledge, skills and values majorly on a self-study basis and constantly seeks means of developing himself. He holds a degree in accounting and is a part-qualified member of the prestigious ACCA.

In 2017, Olanrewaju started out his career as an accounting intern where he learnt the basics of accounting in the real world. He has since grown in the profession after working with Enterprise Hill, a consulting firm that provides accounting and finance solutions to micro and small businesses. Lanre served as the lead consultant at Enterprise Hill from October 2020 to June 2022.

He is passionate about helping businesses build accounting and finance structure that set them up for growth. He loves being part of a team and is always willing to contribute his best to the achievement of the teams common goal.
